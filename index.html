New Vocabualary

Published on: May 13, 2026

In a world obsessed with aesthetics, there is something refreshing about a site that only uses Standard HTML. It loads instantly, works on every device, and focuses entirely on the written word.

"Content is king, but structure is the crown."

To make a blog readable without CSS, you should use:

  • Headings (h1-h6) for hierarchy
  • Horizontal rules (hr) for separation
  • Lists (ul/ol) for organization
